Our Story

Our Story

Mother to Mother started in 2020 in Atteridgeville. Our founder, Sarah Ndlovu, was a new mother who felt very alone after her first baby was born. She wanted help but did not know where to find it.

Sarah met an older mother from her church who gave her advice and support. This changed everything. Sarah saw that other new mothers needed the same help.

She started a small group in her living room. Five mothers came every Tuesday to talk, drink tea, and share advice. The group grew every month.

Our Mission

Mother to Mother connects new mothers with experienced mothers through mentorship and community support groups.

We provide:

  • Free weekly support meetings in local communities
  • One-on-one mentorship from trained mother volunteers
  • Practical advice on baby care, feeding, and self-care
  • A safe space to talk about the challenges of motherhood

Our goal is to make sure no mother feels alone during the first years of her child's life.

To connect experienced mothers with new mothers for guidance, support, and community.
